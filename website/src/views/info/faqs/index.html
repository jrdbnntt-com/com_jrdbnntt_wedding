Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find my RSVP code?

Your RSVP code is located on the back of your invitation. If you cannot find it or are having any other issues RSVPing, please reach out to Jared at jrdbnntt@gmail.com.

What is the dress code?

Please dress semiformal for this summer wedding. Because it is mostly on soft grass, heels are STRONGLY discouraged unless you want to be stuck in the ground.

Are you allowing kids at your wedding?

Unfortunately, we are only able to accommodate children in the wedding party at our reception.

Can I park onsite?

Yes, but there is very limit parking. Please see Parking for more details.

What about COVID?

We hope to have a night that everyone remembers, but not as a superspreader event. As hosts, we are taking all steps to ensure it is as safe as possible, including making it an entirely outdoor event.

We are requiring all guests, including plus ones, to be fully vaccinated and to show proof of vaccination upon arrival.

If resources permit, we will rapid-test all guests. If you are unable or unwilling to be tested or show proof of vaccination, we respectfully ask for you to not attend.

We are currently planning for a full event, but we reserve the right to downsize, reschedule, or cancel in response to the pandemic. Please follow your comfort level and perceptions of risk when choosing to attend. Masks will not be required, but encouraged as a personal choice.

Can I change my RSVP?

You are able to make changes to RSVP, including adjusting your guests, until the RSVP deadline, {{ rsvp_deadline_date }}. To edit your RSVP, go here.

Are plus ones allowed?

In most cases, yes. Each invite is allotted a set number of guests. See How do I change the guests in my RSVP? below for instructions on how to modify your guests.

How do I change the guests in my RSVP?

You can modify your guest list by editing your RSVP until the RSVP deadline, {{ rsvp_deadline_date }}. Once you are done making changes, click the "Save RSVP" button at the bottom of the page.

To add a guest to your RSVP, click the "Add another guest" button and enter the information for the new guest. If you do not see the "Add another guest" button, you have reached the maximum number of guests that your invite has been allotted.

To remove a guest from your reservation, click the "Remove Guest X" button underneath the guest that you would like to remove. You cannot remove the first guest in your reservation, but you can modify the name to make it refer someone else if you want to.
