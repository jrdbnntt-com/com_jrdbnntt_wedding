About the Venue

Getting There

The venue is located at 5708 223rd Ave NE, Redmond, WA 98053.

You will want to arrive by coming from Redmond-Fall City Rd NE and turning onto 218th Ave NE, then left to 219th Ave NE, then right to 56th St which becomes 223rd Ave NE, and then the property will be on your right.

Parking

We recommend using Uber, Lyft or Seattle Yellow Cab.

Parking inside the property gates is extremely limited and reserved for immediate family, vendors, and the wedding party only.

There is limited parking outside the gates available for guests. If you are parking at the venue, we highly recommend carpooling. Please park alongside the property line in the grass at an angle so that we can fit as many cars as possible.

Turski Residence

The venue is a private home and garden owned by the Turski family. The Turskis immigrated from Poland in the late 1980's. Their garden is a monument to over 20 years of patient gardening, and is the ever-growing passion project of Izabella Turska, who transformed the space from a near empty field to the garden oasis that it is today.

The Turski's have graciously opened up their garden to host the wedding. Hannah and Jared hope you enjoy it as much as they do!
